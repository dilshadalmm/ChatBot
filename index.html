Welcome to the first lecture in our series on Quantum Computing. In this introductory session, Dr. Reed breaks down the core principles of quantum mechanics that underpin this revolutionary technology. We'll explore concepts like superposition, entanglement, and quantum bits (qubits).


This course is designed for university students and professionals looking to understand the next wave of computation. No prior knowledge of quantum physics is required, but a background in linear algebra will be beneficial.


Topics Covered:

  • History of Quantum Theory
  • What is a Qubit?
  • Superposition and its implications
  • The mystery of Entanglement
  • Potential applications and future research
